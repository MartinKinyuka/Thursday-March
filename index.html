Scholar Life

At CodeIko students have the exceptional oppurtunity to enhance their lifes both academically and socially. With both student classrooms and rec-rooms, students are given an environment where they can both learn unoffically and officially. Members of CodeIko school are also provided access to a cafeteria for meals and hostels for accomodation.

Day to day activities at the school include but are not limited to daily stand-ups, coding lessons, counselling sessions and evening meet-ups. Daily activities officially begin at six in the morning and run until five in the evening. However, no strict curfew is mandated for these hours and students can use their time of day as they please albeit their course content set for the day must be covered.

Evenings may sometimes consist of recreational activities for the students ranging from karaoke to student organised sports such as basketball. School organised meet-ups/events are mandatory for every CodeIko student and are tailored to improve student experience within the campus.

Club and Activities

Music

The music club is a proud group of thirty students who also comprise the school band. The club has a variety of instrument players and are always looking for new members. The band offers entertainment during school events and are available for hire for corporate events.

Writing

The writer's club is talented group of individual students behind the weekly school gazzette. The collective requires some level of writing skills in order to join and therefore students who wish to join must be vetted rigorously.

Dance

Dance club is an open for all club. Dance is a place to let yourself go whether you are an amateur or a professional dancer. It is by far the most popular club in the school and walk-ins are always encouraged.

Current Students

CodeIko school is a collection of students of various backgrounds and ethncities. The school strives to have a student body that is competent in social skills, coding skills and open-minded to learn as is expected of them. Students at CodeIko school are expected to be neat, respectful and mindful members of the society who shine the light of the school far and beyond.

Alumni Students

CodeIko alumni are excellent potentials for the job market and particularly excel in the fields of data science and programming. The former students can both work in web development and android application development.
